Frequently Asked Questions

What audio formats are supported?

AudioScribe supports all major audio formats including MP3, WAV, M4A, OGG, FLAC, and more. Files can be up to 50MB in size.

How accurate are the transcriptions?

Our transcriptions are powered by OpenAI's Whisper model, which provides industry-leading accuracy. Quality depends on audio clarity, but typically achieves 95%+ accuracy for clear recordings.

What languages are supported?

We support over 50 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Arabic, and Hindi.

Is my audio data secure?

Yes, your privacy is our priority. Audio files are processed securely and automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours. We never store or share your content.

How long does transcription take?

Processing time depends on file length and server load, but most files are transcribed within 2-5 minutes. Longer files may take proportionally more time.

Can I edit the transcription?

You can copy the transcription text and edit it in any text editor. We also provide download functionality to save the transcription as a text file.

What's the maximum file size?

Currently, we support audio files up to 50MB. For larger files, consider compressing the audio or splitting it into smaller segments.

Do you offer bulk transcription?

Currently, our service processes one file at a time. For bulk transcription needs, please contact us for enterprise solutions.
