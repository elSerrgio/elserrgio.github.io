Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

AudioScribe is committed to protecting your privacy. When you use our service, we may collect:

Audio Files

We temporarily process the audio files you upload for transcription purposes. These files are:

  • Processed using secure, industry-standard encryption
  • Automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours of processing
  • Never stored permanently or used for any purpose other than transcription
  • Not accessible to any third parties

Usage Information

We may collect basic usage statistics including:

  • File size and format information
  • Processing time and success rates
  • General geographic location (country level)
  • Browser and device information for compatibility

How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide transcription services
  • Improve service quality and performance
  • Ensure system security and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access controls for processing systems
  • Automatic data deletion after processing

Third-Party Services

We use HuggingFace's infrastructure for AI processing. Your data is subject to their privacy policies during processing, but files are not permanently stored on their systems.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about data processing
  • Request deletion of any stored data
  • Opt out of usage analytics
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@audioscribe.com
